## Magic Minion Tank


**^^Role Responsibilities^^**


⬥ Mage minion tanks are required to lure their minion after killing the amalg.

⬥ If you are tanking umbra you must vuln the first 3 pillars.

⬥ If you are tanking umbra or glacies you must vuln your minion prior to surging

⬥ As any minion you should vuln your amalg.
