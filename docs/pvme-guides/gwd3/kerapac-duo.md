## General Information


This guide assumes you are already familiar with the basic mechanics of Kerapac which can be seen here <a href="" class="inactiveLink">#unknown-channel</a>.



It is recommended to have a Nodon Dragonkin slayer task <img title="slayer" class="d-emoji" alt="slayer" src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/797896049548066857.png?v=1"> as the helmet boost does work. You may also use the Premier Artefact <img title="premierartefact" class="d-emoji" alt="premierartefact" src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/918517285176623104.png?v=1"> to have a 10% chance to not decrease the slayer task count with each kill. Headhunter's thrill bracelets may be used as a switch in order to prolong slayer tasks. The Trophy-Taker's perk also does the same.



Both the Magic/Melee hybrid, and Range duo methods described in this channel are advanced, and cater to high end gear and skill levels.
