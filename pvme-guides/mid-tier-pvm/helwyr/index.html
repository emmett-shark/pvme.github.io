    Helwyr

    Helwyr (Courtesy of #Inflem & #Archeus)

    Introduction

    Helwyr, the Seren general residing in the Heart of Gielinor, requires 80 magic and 40 Seren killcount to fight. The notable drops for this boss are the Cywir Wand , Cywir Orb, and Crest of Seren . These items can be augmented and disassembled for Cywir components which are required for Planted Feet .

    Mechanics

    Attack Rotation

    Mushroom gas → 2 Autos → Cleave → 2 Autos → Frenzy → 2 Autos → Minion Spawn → 2 Autos → Repeat

    Mushroom Gas

    Avoided by just running out of it. Avoid standing directly on top of a mushroom to decrease the distance needed to move out of the gas. Standing in the mushroom gas will give you a 250 damage bleed every tick, and if standing in the gas for a prolonged time will bind you.

    Cleave

    Hits in a frontal cone for up to 3k and apply a bleed. Do not freedom this bleed. If you are standing a distance away from Helwyr, he will first lunge towards you before the attack. There are a few methods to handle this attack.

    ⬥ If using or , move out of the damage cone. Can also be done with but is DPS waste

    ⬥ A well-timed Resonance will heal a nice chunk. Note you will still acquire the bleed. This method is best if you are struggling to Soul Split your HP back up and surviving the whole hour. This method can become difficult with the Cywir Alphas alive, but it can also be a good chance to practice some tick Resonances with little risk as long as you are above 3kish HP.

    ⬥ Lastly, while praying Soul Split , cast / from the Lunar Spellbook.

    Frenzy

    Helwyr’s frenzy attack has him leaping around doing rapid melee damage while applying a bleed stack with each attack. Again, while using Range or Magic, you can just run away from Helwyr’s Frenzy attack, but with Melee it is better to stay put and continue doing damage.

    If you are getting smacked around, you can always pray melee and Devotion or Debilitate . After this attack has concluded, feel free to Freedom the bleed if you wish.

    Minion Spawn

    Helwyr’s minion spawn attack has him howl and summon two 3k HP Cywir Alphas. Easily taken out with AoEs.

    Timing Target Cycle

    To time target cycle you want to use 3 ability from rejoining instance, this is what the Anticipate → Bladed Dive → Sunshine accomplishes

    Tips and Tricks

    ⬥ When you finish a kill, wait until Sunshine is ~4sec off cooldown before rejoining instance.

    ⬥ If you end a kill at <100 adrenaline, teleport out and use Adrenaline Crystal to build.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• If you end at 91-99 adren, use an ability on a wolf instead.

    ⬥ While you have a lot of leeway on Sunshine positioning, you want to ensure you are standing 1 southwest of the middle shroom so that you are Melee Distance to Helwyr from Target Cycle tick.

    ⬥ Ensure to walk two tiles west when walking your combust in the above rotation.

    ⬥ If you get a middle shroom explosion at start, tank it until you finish your first Asphyxiate , then walk 1 more west then adapt to Helwyr's walk before your Detonate to ensure you are still in Sunshine but not taking damage

    ⬥ If you get the shroom explosion that is directly west of middle shroom, then after your first Asphixate walk to another side of your Sunshine to avoid the shroom damage.

    ⬥ If you get both middle and west just teleport out or try to not die.

    ⬥ If you wish to use Vulnerability Bombs , throw it after Sunshine

    Tips and Tricks

    ⬥ Avoid moving unless absolutely necessary, as it will cause Helwyr to skip autos and specs

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Only move Helwyr under the following circumstances:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Walking a bleed

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Avoiding mushroom damage

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Avoiding the frenzy when Devotion is on cooldown (can just Debiliate or face tank it too)

    ⬥ If you get shroomed in your ultimate at the start just teleport out.

    ⬥ Always aim to Resonance the cleave, ideally without protect from melee on.

    ⬥ You can Devotion every other frenzy special.

    ⬥ Ensure to Freedom when it ends, Vulnerbility with every second Freedom you do (this will be the Freedom after the non-Devotioned frenzy).

    ⬥ To time Target Cycle just spam it and run to spot and you will get it, ideally have these binds near one another to make it easy to do

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Refer to #mechanics to set up Target Cycle.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Further, it is important to ensure you run and spam the bind, for if you try to Target Cycle without a target it goes on a cooldown which is reset by walking a tile

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• If you properly Target Cycle Helwyr, his HP box will not show.

    AFK Magic Strategy (~35-39 kph) (Courtesy of #FriggityFrank)

    Gp/hr

    Total 10,190,616

    Note: Assumes Max Reputation and 35 kph.

    Explanation

    ⬥ This method will net less kills per hour but may be used if you do not wish to put high amounts of effort into a mid level boss.

    ⬥ It is important to pray as this method relies on constant healing. It is also important to use the Animate Dead spell, as the method heavily relies on its usage.

    ⬥ Please note this setup/guide is VERY new and is subject to change in the near future.

    ⬥ Aura choice can vary, but will affect how afk kills can be. They are listed in order of effectiveness:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Vampyrism Aura provides extra safety through its healing and allows more chances for poison damage. Due to the constant damage being taken while fighting Helwyr, this is by far the safest and most reliable aura choice to afk with.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Penance Aura allows for the most afk experience, but is less safe to use and doesn't benefit the usage of a Blood Reaver familiar. It is advised to pay attention to your HP with this aura, and to be prepared to eat.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Dark Magic provides extra damage, but you must pay more attention to both your HP and your prayer points. This should be an effective aura for fast kills, but is the least afk choice.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Mahjarrat Aura provides extra damage, but does not give any extra poison procs. Still, it is a viable option as a secondary aura.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Do not use Maniacal for afking, as it will get you killed very easily.

    ⬥ An Inquisitor's Staff will be one of the most effective weapons for this setup due to its passive. If you do not have one, other 2-handed staffs such as Superior Zuriel's or Noxious should be fine to use, but may be slightly slower.

    Mandatory Requirements

    ⬥ This setup should reliably allow you to afk Helwyr, with the duration between checking your status dependent on the aura you are using. However, the following needs to be done or else This method will be either unreliable or will outright not work. Cutting corners will result in failure:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• You are standing 1 step west of the center mushroom

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Adrenaline retention is active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Guthix Book is active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Blood Spell or Exsanguinate is auto-casting

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Animate Dead is unlocked and active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Soulsplit is active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Torment or Affliction is active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Elder Overload is active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Poison is active

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Cinderbane Gloves are equipped

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Blood Reaver is summoned if using Vampyrism Aura

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Ripper Demon is summoned if using other auras

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Auto-retaliate is on

    Preset

    ⬥ Persistent Rage Relic - Prevents adrenaline from draining while out of combat. Can be substituted with an Adrenaline Urn with ocassional stalling.

    ⬥ Kwuarm Incense Sticks - Provide extra poison damage.

    ⬥ Achto Armour - Tank armor is required for Animate Dead to reduce enough damage for this afk method. Can be substituted with Seasinger's armour (Even the tradable version!). Can also replace the top with power armour .

    ⬥ Runes - Runes are of course required to cast Animate Dead and your autocast of choice. The runes you should bring are: , , , , and . Blood Burst/Blitz will be cheaper and slightly safer, while Exsanguinate will be slightly faster but more expensive to use.

    Note: A possible setup is bringing with and then filling with . This way you can switch between Blood Burst/Blitz and Exsanguinate to see which one works best for you.

    ⬥ Essence of Finality - Stored special attack is not used, but is worn for the accuracy bonus and Soulsplit passive.

    ⬥ Reaver's Ring - Extra critical chance helps speed up kills. If not owned or a weaker staff is being used, can be substituted for other strong rings such as Asylum Surgeon's or Ring of Death.

    ⬥ Book of Balance - Decent passive pocket item, provides extra poison procs and damage when activated. The damage contributed by this is actually quite massive, and should be used to ensure the most efficient kills. Reflecting Blood Essence can be used as a budget alternative.
