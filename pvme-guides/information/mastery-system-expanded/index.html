    Mastery system expanded

    Introduction

    Welcome to the expansion of our Mastery System. What it means to be a master varies with each boss and acquiring a Master timer requires a certain level of strategical knowledge. Most if not all of this knowledge can be found within the respective guides; however, we have set out to define what are the most important techniques that a Master should know and use.

    A Master tier player is expected to perform the strategies listed below in their submission. An Adept tier player is expected to attempt the majority of the listed strategies in their submission.

    Nex, Angel of Death

    Master Requirement

    @AoD Master - Average sub 2:00 across 3 b2b 7man kills (subject to vod review)

    Current Masters: 2

    Ranged Ent

    ⬥ Prebuild with Incendiary Shot and Death's Swiftness .

    ⬥ P1

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ECB Spec at 4.8s, target cycle on tick with the proper rotation.

    ⬥ P2

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Pre-Snipe phase 2 on tick.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Proper minion lure.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Stand 2 steps out from usual Surge spot if Umbra / Glacies, reluring if blocked.

    ⬥ P3

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ChinSGB under ECB followed by at least Rapid Fire and Snap Shot on phase 3.

    ⬥ P4 & Pillars

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Good pillar movement and handling of bombs / instakills.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Good ability priorization on Zaros.

    Chinner

    ⬥ Pre Incendiary Shot / Meteor Strike and Death's Swiftness

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Not applicable to 2/4.

    ⬥ P1

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Hammer is applied.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ECB with proper ability usage.

    ⬥ P2

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ECB with pre-Snipe and Snap Shot .

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Storm Shards are applied.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Death's Swiftness as soon as possible.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Phase to 1.8 before getting on the Amalgs.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Help Amalgs as needed.

    ⬥ P3

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Death's Swiftness build to 100 with Adrenaline potion and Hydrix Bakriminels .

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Proper Chincend.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Equipping your regular weapons afterwards.

    ⬥ P4 & Pillars

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Don't lose ticks.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Good bomb handling.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Proper ability priorization.

    Melee

    ⬥ P1

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Tag correctly, fix if possible.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Natural Instinct and DBA Spec inside the instance is optimal, build quickly.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Camping a non TMW piece is quite significant for the Vengeance .

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- It makes juicing Dharok's relic slightly harder, but camping Soul Split for Vengeance is superior damage.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Make sure your Ripper is targetting Nex.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Correct rotation, get 2h autos from Freedom , and use 2h Assault .

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Flicking appropriately to avoid camping high HP.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• After boss phases on Cleave , apply a Storm Shard .

    ⬥ P2

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• A Master should be able to know when phase 1 phased a tick or two late.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Correct rotation: → defensive → Greater Barge → stall , release after 2 ticks with Cleave → Flank → Hurricane AoD and the Amalg.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Can stall Cleave instead of GFlurry if you dont have enough adrenaline.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• After the Amalg dies, stall an ability such as Quake and release with 188, Bladed Dive to aggro the minion and Surge minion if the opportunity arises.

    ⬥ P3

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Correct rotation.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Wielding Scythe or going melee distance of minions with ZGS for Cleave / Hurricane

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Realizing if it’s worth skipping the auto attack at end of Berserk rotation.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Place north smoke on midline and east smoke 1 tile south of mid or 2 tiles north of mid. Don't smoke the team.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Spam your Ripper.

    ⬥ P4 & Pillars

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Greater Ricochet swap, this allows you to have enough adrenaline for Pillars.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Avoid Havoc on pillars entirely.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Dont dump adren unnecessarily.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Call Rippers on Pillars.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Don’t lose time moving between pillars or when going to mid for the instakill.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Keep an eye on Pillar HP and try to determine if the pillar will die during your GCD or not. If it will, then Surge + Bladed Dive to next pillar but also dont Surge away from the pillars early.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Proper 600k rotation which would involve Invigorate 4 for a high chance at a 3-threshold Berserk

    Raksha

    Master Requirement

    @Raksha Master - Average 2:20 min/kill across 2 b2b kills, solo or duo (duo must be base role)

    Current Masters: 3

    General Notes

    ⬥ Bolt switch and improvise as needed.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• If you're low adrenaline and you're going to Greater Ricochet, switch to Hydrix .

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• If you're high adrenaline and going to use Greater Ricochet, switch to Rubies .

    ⬥ Vulnerability bomb ‎ ‎are thrown appropriately.

    ⬥ When using Eldritch crossbow , don't camp protection prayers for too long, flicking ‎ ‎as needed.

    ⬥ Usage of Vengeance / Disruption shield

    ⬥ Incendiary shot the dummy correctly + Bladed dive to instance without losing ticks.

    ⬥ Be mindful of your HP when deciding to dodge or intentionally tank rocks for Dharok's relic.

    Phase 1

    ⬥ Proper rotation is followed

    ⬥ Tag appropriately.

    ⬥ Get the 2h auto when using Shadow Tendrils

    ⬥ Stay in your Death’s Swiftness while managing rocks.

    ⬥ Call your Ripper demon if it gets stuck.

    Phase 2

    ⬥ Stay in your Death’s Swiftness while managing rocks

    ⬥ Walk your Fragmentation shot properly.

    ⬥ Proper ability improvisation is followed.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• For instance, Snipe over Tuska's wrath depending on the HP needed for the skip accounting for bleeds.

    Phase 3

    ⬥ No ticks lost when clearing blobs.

    ⬥ Proper ability priorization.

    ⬥ The minion is completely ignored.

    Phase 4

    ⬥ Rapid anima absorption.

    ⬥ Call your Ripper demon if it gets stuck.

    Rise of the Six

    Master Requirement

    @RotS Master - 46 kill hour (Incite, subject to vod review)

    Current Masters: 10

    Tunneling

    ⬥ Knowing how to maneuver around tunnels and make efficient use of Powerburst of Acceleration potions.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Awareness of Surge spots and important rock locations.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Not dropping rocks.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Drink an Acceleration pot every 2nd tunnel in sync with the team.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Be aware of who will be making each subsequent instance

    Follow the Proper Rotation

    ⬥ Proper rotations followed, which depends on what rotation it is that day

    ⬥ Proper use of positioning with regards to starting and running.

    ⬥ Manage your adrenaline, with appropriate use of Adrenaline renewal and Combat dummies

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• This includes reacting to occasional fast kills where you wont have time to build adrenaline back up.

    Specials

    Should optimally never really see specials, at most you will probably see 2 Karil specs

    ⬥ Minimum number of specs will be seen when you properly learn to two-step and follow the rotation

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Assuming you see a spec it really depends how it is dealt with as well

    ⬥ Assuming you see a Karil spin side shouldnt be more than 21s

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Eldritch crossbow → Snipe → Greater Ricochet

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Time Snipe to hit as soon as Karil becomes susceptible after spinning.

    ⬥ If you see an Ahrim spin that is a very bad sign.

    ⬥ Assuming bomb should still be sub 20, depending on how the kill is going.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Hopping sides is an important factor to keep in mind, specially if its near the end of the kill and you are East.

    ⬥ Reacting to Karil spec on the opposite side, watching out for lightning, not getting hit by bombs and attack Karil on ledge.

    Vorago

    Master Requirement

    @Vorago Master - Sub 9:10 average over 2 b2b Duo kills on any week (must show exp drops)

    Current Masters: 12

    Phase 1

    ⬥ Solo Lure (base)

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• A strong rotation is expected.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Using a Natural Instinct rotation (dummy at face) is recommended but not required.

    ⬥ Hammer Climb (bomb)

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Climb should be done cleanly.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Base should not take the red bomb.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Acceleration pot climb is not required.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Accel pot climb can save around 1-4 ticks with a higher execution difficult for both you and your base (rotation).

    ⬥ Post-jump rotations should be adjusted in relation to hp remaining.

    Phase 2

    ⬥ Storm Shards

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• 10 is expected, at a minimum 7.

    ⬥ Vorago should be consistently debuffed.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Statius's warhammer + Quake

    ⬥ Rotating Barricade + Intercept properly

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Base should step out of melee distance if random red bomb.

    ⬥ Vorago should be damaged on the tick he stands after Bring Him Down.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Shatter should be on tick.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• The boss should be vulned for this part.

    ⬥ Combat dummy should be placed correctly at the end of phase according to the weekly rotation.

    Phase 3

    ⬥ A Natural Instinct rotation is required.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• should be timed correctly.

    ⬥ Pre-Barge dummy + stall Statius's warhammer (base)

    ⬥ Pre-Eldritch crossbow (range) + stall an ability (bomb)

    ⬥ Should not lose ticks throughout phase.

    Phase 4

    ⬥ Shard off cooldown.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Number is variable depending on week.

    ⬥ Vorago should be consistently debuffed.

    ⬥ Statius's warhammer + Quake

    ⬥ Stone Clone Skip should be executed correctly.

    ⬥ Rotation after last Waterfall.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Shatter

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• The boss should be under Vulnerability for this part.

    ⬥ Combat Dummy should be placed correctly at the end of phase.

    ⬥ Natural Instinct for p5 should be timed perfectly.

    Phase 5

    ⬥ Damage boosting ultimate should be timed correctly.

    ⬥ A 0 special rotation should be attempted.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Full 0 special push is not required.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Should push atleast 4 tiles before Vorago's 1st attack.

    Solak

    Grandmaster Requirement

    @Solak Grandmaster - Sub 5:40 Duo (-10s if storm tanking)

    Current Grandmasters: 4

    Phase 1

    ⬥ DPS does 2 Eldritch Crossbow before skip.

    ⬥ Zaros Godsword with Magic / Melee is used after your Berserk .

    ⬥ Make use of a Combat dummy & Mechanized chinchompas for faster legs.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Auto-fail if this is not done.

    ⬥ An appropiate Core rotation is followed.

    Phase 2

    ⬥ Efficient South & North Eruptions.

    ⬥ Calorie Bomb guidelines:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• → → - No Calorie Bomb.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• → → - Calorie Bomb is used.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- The / may not be needed if your ability priorization was good enough.

    ⬥ It's beneficial to do Natural Instinct → Divert for Range / Melee hybrid, as much as Ranged camp to allow a Natural Instinct → Incendiary Shot → Divert for rapid Phase 3's.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• This has the advantage of allowing Adrenaline Renewal on your Death's Swiftness in P3 for Melee / Range hybrid.

    Phase 3

    ⬥ Natural Instinct → Divert allows bled Assault → 5t Flurry → Destroy and can still finish Berserk on 100% for instant Death's Swiftness

    ⬥ Using Vengeance on the pad bomb hit will damage Erethdor directly.

    ⬥ As a DPS, Death's Swiftness is placed North.

    ⬥ Base lures South to allow Flanking

    ⬥ Dealing with the stun mechanic before it actually happens.

    ⬥ Instant pad p3.

    ⬥ A near instant mind entry.

    ⬥ A good ability priorization on Erethdor.

    P4

    ⬥ Barge on tick.

    ⬥ Adjust based off damage dealt to either do:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• 3t Flurry → Destroy / Shatter → Destroy / Shatter

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Stall Hurricane → Flurry

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• If over 350k+ you may need to stall Assault + Cleave

    ⬥ Storm Shards should have been used enough throughout the previous phases to be detonated for good damage in this phase.

    ⬥ Using Vengeance on the bomb hit.

    ⬥ Making use of Dharok's relic by being low HP.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Wrath as a last ditch attempt for non-ECB .

    Telos

    Grandmaster Requirement

    @Telos Grandmaster - Sub 3:50 (Magic) or Sub 4:00 (Melee) or Sub 3:15 (Range) average over 3 b2b kills at 2449%+

    Current Grandmasters: 5

    Magic

    ⬥ Phase 1

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Lure correctly so that you clear tendrils and can surge to jump spot without wasting time

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Adjusting rotation to damage/hp threshold (e.g. make use of divert/res before charging deto so that you can save your ds for P2)

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Knowing when to Vengeance

    ⬥ Phase 2

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Vengeance and Disruption shield appropriately.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Lure Telos correctly so that it’s not in the beam and your Ripper demon can attack without getting stuck.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Ripper demon juicing via drinking Spiritual prayers

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Adjusting rotation to damage / HP threshold

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Replacing 2nd Asphyxiate with Dragon breath is an example of this.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Stall ability after meeting HP threshold if the opportunity occurs

    ⬥ Phase 3

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Get into the beam fast

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Adjusting rotation to damage / HP threshold

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Auto + Detonate Guthix staff VS auto + Detonate Asphyxiate

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Stall ability after meeting HP threshold if the opportunity occurs.

    ⬥ Phase 4

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Get into the fonts as fast as possible.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Avoid over-stunning Telos after meeting HP threshold.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Avoid being overly defensive walking into 2nd font.
