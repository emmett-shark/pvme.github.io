    Telos melee

    Telos with Melee (Courtesy of #Evil Lucario)

    Introduction

    Why Melee?

    Melee at Telos is the safest style to use at the highest skill level. You can gain benefits such as less RNG needed on volcanic golems, KO protection, and the best balance between complete safety and speed. There are some precise actions needed, but not that much.

    This guide assumes 250%+ and work best at 1000%+, but these rotations still work below 250% with some tweaks. Adjust accordingly from there - below 250%, these rotations can be loosely followed but after that, you definitely want to make sure you follow them.

    An average kill at 2449% enrage is worth 15,942,553 in uniques alone. This assumes an estimated orb price of 193,723,482. Advanced players can get up to 12 kph, earning 191,310,631/hr.

    Notable Enrage Checkpoints and Changes past 999%

    ⬥ 1000%: P4 one rock will fall, P5 multiple rocks will fall, immortality no longer works P5, and Telos will freedom stuns and binds with a 16 tick cooldown

    ⬥ 2000%: "So you think you can guard?" Achievement, aka Silver Warden

    ⬥ 2700%: Tendrils damage required caps at 30,000

    ⬥ 3300%: The first prayer drain on P3 will smite you from full unless Telos touches the beam

    ⬥ 4000%: Hard enrage cap; "The True Warden" Achievement, aka Gold Warden

    Phase 1

    Throughout 250% to 4000%, the P1 rotation will always remain the same. The big objectives are to block and reduce as much damage as possible without using many defensives and to apply a Barge bleed + Hurricane on tendrils perfectly.

    Random defensive + two ticks after → → target cycle + + → [ if you get a Relentless proc on ] → → → → [Skip if you got a Relentless proc] → → → → → → s → r + →

    If your Divert splashes completely, just use an adrenaline renewal . Divert also works much better at higher enrages, but it's still good enough for lower enrages above 500% or so. This lets you save your adrenaline renewal for emergencies if it works out. Take off your platebody for a better chance of Divert working.

    That stalled Assault is important so you line up two Assault hits and Hurricane all on top of each other for tendrils, nearly guaranteeing an instant free or a tick-late free even past 2700% enrage. This is less important at lower enrages and you don't need to stall at lower enrages, but if you want to do enrages above 2000% it is highly recommended you learn how to do it.

    During the Cleave and Decimate after the initial Berserk , you should be running out of range to bait out Telos’s Magic attacks. This is super important because Telos’s Magic attacks hit far less than his Melee attacks, which makes it much easier to survive when under Berserk , especially as you approach 4000%.

    After the Cleave after Hurricane , it depends entirely on how much LP Telos has. If he hits 450k right after Cleave , wait until the tick Telos phases, then use Quake + Bladed Dive to the drop-off point. That Quake is important because it lets you carry over that damage to the next phase immediately, giving you a noticeable headstart for P2. This works because AoE abilities like Quake , Bombardment , and Chain calculate their damage late enough to be forced into the next phase if you use them on the same tick as phasing to the next phase. Practically speaking, only Quake can be used like this as Cleave hits too quickly.

    If Telos is not immediately phased after that Cleave , do strong basics like Decimate and Sever to get it down before uppercut happens, then Quake prephase if it lines up nicely. If you do get uppercut, phase him immediately after and prephase with a Quake .

    Phase 2

    The start of P2 depends on how many autoattacks Telos has before Onslaught and how much adrenaline you have going into P2. If you have over 77% adrenaline, you will want to ZGS spec before bleeding Greater Flurry . Otherwise, reverse that order.

    If 2 autos before Onslaught at 77%+ adren:

    Wait a tick → (dw) → → → bleed + same tick → s + back into ZGS spec → r + + throw right as Telos autos → → → basics → → → → basics until 300k → prephase

    If 2 autos before Onslaught under 77% adren:

    Wait a tick → → bleed → → + same tick → s + back into ZGS spec → r + + throw right as Telos autos → → → basics → → → → basics until 300k → prephase

    Waiting that one tick before Barge is extremely important, because if you just Barge instantly you will deal no damage. So wait a tick for that damage immunity to pass by. An easy way to time this is to spam click a , then Barge immediately after you see your prayer points be restored.

    The Escape and Bladed Dive back in are done to ensure Telos leaves the black beam with zero RNG. It must be perfectly done on the same tick as your other ability though, as anything less than perfect play like being late will cause the lure to fail and you will have to end up blocking the black beam. Focus on getting the Escape , as the Bladed Dive back is far more lenient.

    If 0-1 autos before Onslaught at 77%+ adren:

    Wait a tick → (dw) → → → → + drag Telos out of the beam → → → basics → → → → basics until 300k → prephase

    If 0-1 autos before Onslaught under 77% adren:

    Wait a tick → → → → → + drag Telos out of the beam → → → basics → → → → basics until 300k → prephase

    Try to get Assault in as early as possible, and try to aim to phase before Hold Still as well if you can.

    If after uppercut on P1 OR no ZGS:

    Wait a tick → → → (5taa) + → + → → + walk → → → → → basics and thresholds until 300k → prephase

    Ideally you should not get this, but the above rotation deals with it correctly. If you don't have a ZGS, just use more basics between Assault and Freedom .

    The autoattack with 2h after Destroy is known as “5TAA”, or “5T” for short. Basically, after a dual wield ability, you can switch to your 2h weapon and force out a quick autoattack two ticks after that ability. Because of the stun duration, this 5T wastes just enough time yet still gains damage to allow for the longest stun duration from a bled Destroy to Backhand to Forceful Backhand . This will get used in both P3 and P4, so even if you don’t get uppercut on P1 to force this rotation, it’s worth learning this.

    Phase 3

    The goal in Phase 3 is to burst down Telos as quickly and consistently as possible.

    Wait a tick → → → (5taa) + → + into the beam → → → + walk → → → basics to 150k

    You don’t necessarily need to Quake prephase here because P4 is somewhat time-locked anyway, but it's helpful to. If you don’t have enough adrenaline to fit in both Blood Tendrils and Slaughter before Assault, just skip Blood Tendrils and do some basics instead.

    In order to make P4 better at higher enrages so you aren’t scrambling for adrenaline, you can lure Telos into the beam and balance the bar. That way, when Telos drains you right after Assault , it won't completely deplete your adrenaline. Should the beam spawn on Telos, do this conservatively, else you will have to delay your Assault by one ability so you can use Disruption Shield on the explosion losslessly.

    Phase 4

    The goal here is to do as much damage as possible to Telos before he can fire off any special attacks, so rocks are completely skipped.

    Wait a tick → → → (5taa) + → →

    Use Limitless here if you can. If your adrenaline is really dire, skip Statius’s warhammer and Quake instead.

    For font 1, Resonance + Reflect and build to 100% adrenaline. Wait for the chatbox message “Telos: You dare to defy me?” and throw a sticky bomb on the golems right when that happens. Get out of range from the golems, then Barge Telos right as he gets up and throws the instant-kill.

    Safe Rotation (0 Telos autos):

    Telos + run back to font → → wait 2 ticks, (5taa if possible) + → → or if you missed it earlier

    Fast Rotation (1 Telos auto):

    Telos + run back to font → → 3 hit → or or if you missed it earlier

    Safest Rotation (1-2 Telos autos depending on Barge timing):

    Telos right as the instant-kill lands → → → (5taa) + → → or or if you missed it earlier

    For this safest rotation, you need to use Barge perfectly on tick, otherwise you will either get instant-killed or you will get two Telos autos. In addition, if sub-1000% and you didn't pop Freedom, immediately bleed Destroy and skip Decimate .

    For font 2, do the same thing except you’re tanking everything here, so be prepared to eat up a lot. This time, use Revenge with defender . Skip Revenge if you are not using a defender . Use the same cues as in font 1 and go to town. You can use Devotion here to mitigate damage, but if you’re going for fast font 3, skip that.

    After second instant-kill, Telos right as the instant-kill lands. This always pops Telos’s Freedom immediately at 1000%+, delaying his next action. Bleed → 3 hit → for safety, otherwise just use a basic for more adrenaline if you are confident in your DPS. Below 1000%, his Freedom may not be back on time, so you can skip bleeding Greater Flurry and just bleed Destroy immediately.

    Safe Font 3:

    After Telos phases into font 3, turn the corner around the middle beam area and wait for all the golems to line up single-file. Bladed Dive over to the end of the group of three golems and use Ingenuity of the Humans + Entangle on the closest golem. This traps all golems behind that one golem for 24 seconds, which is more than enough to deal with Telos himself.

    Resonance and Reflect the font, then ZGS spec + adrenaline potion . Barge him with Mobile , then stall a Cleave . Release the Cleave two ticks after the message “Telos is preparing to fire an anima bomb!” and bleed Assault right after. Follow that up with either Destroy (since it can stun out of range), or Backhand → Cleave or Destroy and finish the job there. Alternatively, just bleed Destroy right after instant-kill, 5taa to Backhand then Forceful Backhand .

    Fast Font 3 (very dangerous!):

    Immediately head into font 3 and start eating up while using Resonance + Reflect . Right at the end of the font, use Devotion and lure the golems away from Telos. Sticky bomb them OR use Ingenuity of the Humans + Corruption Shot with bolas and Barge Telos with Mobile → Assault → Destroy → basics until dead while keeping minions at bay with more sticky bombs. Extremely dangerous however, and not recommended unless you are very confident.

    Phase 5

    Make sure you have Disruption Shield active and are praying Melee at the start of the phase. If you do the rest of this phase perfectly, Telos and golems cannot KO you at the start of the phase no matter what, as Disruption Shield will always prioritize blocking Telos's Magic attack.

    Make sure you you click on Telos with your mouse while doing defensives before , as you gain lossless autos. These autos may add up in damage helping to clear post virus / on virus clear much easier. Also remember to click away from telos at least 4.8 seconds (8 ticks) before using , otherwise you'd lose bleed buff.

Newcomers to this enrage/rotation should start with either Immortality or dragon battleaxe first, only using Berserk in the above rotation if you're confident in surviving.

    These have zero RNG with getting stunned at inopportune times. The only risk is a potential rock before Barricade , but that’s very rare. You can also afford to swap Statius’s warhammer with Freedom on side beam for that bit of extra safety if volcanics took their time to start hitting you at the start. And as before, if you are not confident with volcanics out there, you can simply use Immortality or dragon battleaxe instead of Berserk , which is even more advised to use over Berserk .

    It’s also a near necessity to wait those two ticks before Barge as well and nail the timing. If you Barge too early, golems can surround you when inside the beam and it can hurt far more, and if you Barge too late your Disruption Shield can no longer guarantee you not getting KOed at the start.

    Phase 4 General Notes

    ⬥ Sticky bombs take 3 ticks to land when thrown, and creates a 3x3 area that will make bindable/stunnable enemies get stuck.

    ⬥ Phase Telos to hp cap then run into the appropriate font.

    ⬥ Once font is depleted click on sticky bomb and use the right click option on middle golem. Don't drop sticky yet.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Throw the first sticky bomb when you get the popup message You dare defy me?

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Throw the second sticky bomb directly beyond the first sticky bomb when you get the popup message Telos is preparing to fire an anima bomb!

    ⬥ When you barge and start damaging telos, put yourself in a direct line beyond the minions and the sticky bombs so they stay in line and run into the next sticky bomb rather than dispersing and running around the bomb.

    Note: there are pre-barge and non pre-barge strategies to damage telos between fonts. This section will have examples showing pre-barge.
