    Upgrade order

    Introduction

    This channel lists the general natural combat style progression template for players to follow.

    Disclaimer

    Priorities can of course change depending on content being done by the player. You should note that when you upgrade weapons, you can sell the weapon being upgraded to deduct cost from the new weapon.

    For example, once you are ready to obtain Blightbound Crossbows , you can sell one or both of your Ascensions for a good chunk of change. Keep in mind that the old off-hand Ascension Crossbow can be used as a Flanking switch, so you may want to keep that specifically to Flank.

    You should also feel free to buy items that are stated as less important in the upgrade order, such as buying Laceration Boots which costs 1,639,566 before the Zaros Godsword , which currently costs ‎ ‎ 863,354,747.

    Feel free to ask in #unknown-channel for a tailored list for the content that you do!
